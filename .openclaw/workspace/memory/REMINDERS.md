# Marketing Campaign Reminders

## Weekly Check-ins
Every Monday:
- Review website traffic and conversion rates
- Monitor delivery order volume trends
- Track customer retention metrics
- Analyze social media engagement
- Assess ad campaign performance

## Monthly Reviews
Last day of each month:
- Compare metrics against baseline
- Update tracking dashboards
- Identify areas needing optimization
- Document key findings and recommendations

## Quarterly Goals Review
End of each quarter:
- Evaluate progress towards Q3/Q4 2025 goals
- Refine targets based on data
- Adjust strategies as needed

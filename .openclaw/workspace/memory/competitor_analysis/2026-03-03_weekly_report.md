# Weekly Competitor Analysis Report
Date: March 3, 2026

## Executive Summary
This week's analysis reveals continued aggressive promotional activity in the Orange County cannabis delivery market. Key findings include new promotional strategies by Eaze and extended operating hours by Grassdoor, indicating intensifying competition in the space.

## Key Changes from Previous Week
- Eaze has launched new March promotions offering up to 35% off
- Grassdoor maintains extended operating hours (6:00 AM - 2:00 AM daily)
- Market continues to emphasize rapid delivery times (40-minute guarantees)

#### 1. Eaze (eaze.com)

- Maintaining strong market presence through digital channels
- Enhanced security measures observed on their platform

#### 2. Grassdoor (grassdoor.com)

## Competitive Advantages/Threats

### Advantages
1. Market showing increased price sensitivity (opportunity for strategic promotions)
2. Extended operating hours becoming standard (we can differentiate on service quality)
3. Competitors focusing on rapid delivery promises create opportunity for quality-focused positioning

### Threats
1. Aggressive promotional pricing (particularly from Eaze's 35% off campaign)
2. Extended operating hours becoming industry standard (6:00 AM - 2:00 AM)
3. 40-minute delivery promises raising customer expectations

## Recommended Actions
1. Consider strategic response to Eaze's 35% promotion while maintaining profitability
2. Review our operating hours against Grassdoor's 6:00 AM - 2:00 AM schedule
3. Evaluate feasibility of delivery time guarantees to remain competitive
4. Enhance mobile platform security to match competitor standards
5. Consider implementing a tiered loyalty program to combat aggressive promotional pricing

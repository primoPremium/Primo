# Safe Competitor Analysis Procedures

## Approved Research Methods

1. Physical Market Research
- Mystery shopping through regular customer channels
- In-person visits where applicable
- Phone inquiries as normal customers
- Collection of physical marketing materials

2. Public Information Sources
- Company press releases
- Public financial reports (if available)
- News articles
- Legal business registrations
- Public job postings

3. Social Media Monitoring
- Official social media accounts
- Public posts and engagement
- Advertised promotions
- Customer service interactions

4. Customer Feedback Analysis
- Public review sites
- Customer testimonials
- Social media mentions
- Community forums (publicly available)

## Safe Data Collection Methods

1. Customer Experience Research
   - Place legitimate orders
   - Document natural shopping experience
   - Note delivery times and service
   - Save receipts and promotional materials

2. Marketing Material Collection
   - Subscribe to email newsletters
   - Follow social media accounts
   - Keep physical marketing materials
   - Document public advertisements

3. Price Monitoring
   - Regular legitimate purchases
   - Save receipts
   - Track promotional offers
   - Note seasonal variations

1. Week 1: Internal Analysis
   - Website performance

   - Promotion effectiveness

2. Week 2: Marketing Material Review

   - Social media content

3. Week 3: Customer Experience

4. Week 4: Strategy Review
